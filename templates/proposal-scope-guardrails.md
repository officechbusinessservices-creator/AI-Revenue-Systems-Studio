# Proposal Scope Guardrails

## Included by Default
- Strategy + implementation for agreed channels
- Documentation and training handoff
- KPI dashboard setup + reporting cadence

## Excluded by Default
- Custom software/app development
- Paid media buying/management
- Full rebrand/design overhaul
- Additional channels not listed in scope

## Change Control
- Any out-of-scope request becomes a change request
- Change requests are estimated and approved in writing
- Timeline adjustments apply to approved scope changes

## Revision Policy
- Fixed revision rounds per deliverable
- Additional revisions billed separately

## SLA Expectations
- Client approval SLA: ___ business days
- Studio response SLA: ___ business days
- Escalation contact and path documented
